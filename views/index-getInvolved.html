Get Involved

Join Our Facebook Group

Our Facebook group is the place to connect with fellow campers in the Indianapolis area. Stay up to date on activities, ask for help on a coding project, show a blog post, etc.

Come to a Meetup

Join our Meetup Group and come to a meeting. We hold workshops, hackathons, talks and more. Everyone from beginners to experts are welcome.

Start or Contribute to a Project

Check out our Project Page. If you see an open project that interests you, start contributing. Or start your own project.

Add your Blog

Do you have your own coding blog? Submit a link to be added to our blog list page or a specific article to be considered for our blog of the month.

Speak at a Meetup

If you have knowledge of a topic that you think may be of interest or would be beneficial for our FCC Indy members, let us know. We are always looking for speakers for our Meetups.
